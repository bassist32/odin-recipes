Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A super easy recipe for a hearty Italian classic. Whether you're cooking for family, friends, or a loved one it's guaranteed to be a hit!

Whilst we prefer spaghetti, you can pair this delicious meaty sauce with any pasta of your choice

Ingredients (Serves 6)

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon, finely choppped
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2-3 sprigs rosemary leaves, picked and finely chopped
  • 500g beef mince
  • 400g spaghetti (or pasta of your choice)

    • For the bolognese sauce

  • 2x 400g tins chopped tomatoes
  • small pack basil leaves, picked and finely chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 fresh bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped (if using)
  • 125ml red wine (your choice!)

    • To season and serve (optional)

  • 75g parmesan, grated
  • 6 basil leaves
  • crusty bread

Method

  1. Place a large saucepan on medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil.
  2. Once the oil has heated up, add 4 finely chopped bacon rashers and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp.
  3. Reduce the heat, add the 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves and the leaves from 2-3 sprigs of rosemary, all finely chopped. Fry until the veg softens (about 10 mins).
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high, add 500g beef mince and cook, stirring often, until the meat is browned all over.
  5. Add 2 tins chopped tomatoes, the finely chopped basil leaves, 1 tsp dried oregano, 2 bay leaves, 2 tbsp tomatoo purée, 1 beef stock cube, 1 deseeded and finely chopped red chili (if using), 125ml red wine.
  6. Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for 1hr 15mins stirring occasionally, until you have a rich, thick sauce.
  7. Add the 75g grated parmesan. Taste to check the seasoning and stir.
  8. When the bolognese is nearly finished, cook 400g of your pasta of choice following the pack instructions.
  9. Drain the pasta, and serve together with the bolognese sauce.
